A little bit about me...

I am a student in The University of Michigan's Master of Information program, and soon to be UX researcher & designer. I love gaining knowledge, working with new technology, and reading autobiographies. I'm originally from Ypsilanti, Michigan. My favorite hobby is traveling to the world's best beaches and watching the sunrise. My dream job is not at a specific company, but at one that focuses on the user above anything else. I currently live in Westland, Michgan, which is 30 minutes outside of Detroit, Michigan. I want to be a UX researcher & designer because providing answers to the most challenging questions in product design seems amazing. My favorite quote is “Success is liking yourself, liking what you do, and liking how you do it.” - Maya Angelou.
